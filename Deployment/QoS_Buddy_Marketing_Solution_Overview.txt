QoS BUDDY
AI-powered network quality intelligence for modern NOC teams

Marketing Solution Overview


1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

QoS Buddy is an AI-powered network operations solution that helps teams monitor
live quality-of-service signals, detect anomalies, forecast service degradation,
diagnose likely root causes, recommend safer actions, and preserve incident
knowledge in one unified command center.

Instead of giving operators another passive dashboard, QoS Buddy connects the
entire operational flow: observe, detect, diagnose, predict, optimize, report,
and learn. It is designed for telecom operators, enterprise network teams,
managed service providers, NOC leaders, and AI engineering teams that need to
protect service quality while making AI outputs understandable, governed, and
useful in real operations.

Core promise:
See quality problems sooner, understand why they happen, and choose the safest
next action with traceable evidence.


2. WHAT QOS BUDDY DOES

QoS Buddy turns raw network telemetry into actionable intelligence.

The platform ingests live QoS and network performance metrics such as latency,
jitter, throughput, packet loss, radio indicators, queue length, active
connections, quality scores, anomaly signals, and related context. These signals
flow through specialized AI agents that detect abnormal behavior, forecast
future risk, identify likely causes, retrieve similar past incidents, recommend
actions, and summarize the current state for operators and decision-makers.

The result is a practical AI command center for network quality operations.


3. THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES

Network operations teams often face the same painful pattern:

- Problems become visible only after users experience service degradation.
- Monitoring tools show symptoms, but not always causes or next actions.
- AI models are often isolated in notebooks and not connected to live workflows.
- Incident knowledge is scattered across logs, tickets, reports, and individual
  experience.
- Manual triage takes too long during moments when every minute matters.
- Leaders need clear summaries, while engineers need technical evidence.

QoS Buddy addresses these gaps by connecting live monitoring, machine learning,
incident memory, decision governance, and reporting into one operational system.


4. CORE FEATURES

Live QoS Monitoring
QoS Buddy consumes real-time network and host metrics to maintain a live view of
service quality. It helps operators track latency, jitter, packet loss,
throughput, radio quality, traffic load, and other performance indicators from a
single command center.

Anomaly Detection
The detection layer scores incoming metrics and raises alerts when behavior
deviates from expected patterns. This helps teams identify service degradation
earlier and focus attention on the signals that matter.

Prediction and Forecasting
The prediction agent analyzes recent metric windows and forecasts future risk.
It can estimate likely QoS degradation and time-to-breach, helping teams move
from reactive monitoring to proactive intervention.

Root-Cause Diagnosis
The diagnostic layer turns alerts into structured root-cause context. It helps
operators understand likely causes, supporting evidence, and the operational
meaning behind a technical event.

Optimization Recommendations
The optimization agent evaluates possible remediation actions and records
decisions with traceable evidence. This supports safer, policy-aware operations
instead of rushed, ad hoc remediation.

Incident Memory and RAG
QoS Buddy uses ChromaDB as incident memory for similar-case retrieval. The
system can connect current issues to past incidents, making lessons learned
available during future triage.

MLflow Observability
Prediction and optimization activity can be logged to MLflow with runs, metrics,
traces, and artifacts. This gives teams evidence for model behavior, decision
quality, and auditability.

Role-Aware Dashboard
QoS Buddy provides an operator-facing dashboard designed for both executive and
engineering views. Leaders get clear status and decisions; engineers can inspect
technical evidence, model outputs, and operational details.

Reporting and Synthesis
The platform aggregates live state into concise summaries and reports. This
helps teams communicate incidents, actions, and outcomes without manually
assembling information from multiple tools.

6. WHY QOS BUDDY IS DIFFERENT

QoS Buddy is not just a dashboard and not just an AI model. It is an operational
AI workflow.

It is agent-based.
Each capability has a clear role: monitoring, detection, prediction, diagnosis,
optimization, synthesis, and reporting.

It is event-driven.
Live telemetry activates downstream intelligence automatically, allowing the
system to respond continuously as conditions change.

It is evidence-oriented.
Predictions, diagnoses, actions, and reports are supported by metrics, model
outputs, incident memory, and traceable artifacts.

It is operator-centered.
The experience is designed around NOC workflows, not around isolated data
science experiments.

It is practical to deploy.
The stack is containerized and designed for a local or portable demonstration
environment, while preserving the architecture needed for larger deployments.
